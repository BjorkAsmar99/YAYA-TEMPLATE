# USER.md - User Context & Preferences

User: Baramine

Core Mission:
Baramine creates and sells premium digital experiences (not simple websites) to individuals and professionals. Every project must feel unique, cinematic, emotionally engaging, and visually magnificent.

Non-Negotiable Quality Standards:
- Sweet, elegant, cinematic landing page choreography with refined scroll-triggered reveals and soft grain textures (oddritualgolf.com level)
- Unique, pleasurable, spatial navigation systems with magnetic interactions and delightful browsing momentum (igloo.inc level)
- Deeply immersive storytelling that transforms the site into an architectural/cinematic journey with sophisticated image treatments and parallax layering (mersi-architecture.com/projets/paix level)
- All animations must use organic spring physics or premium cubic-bezier curves and feel emotionally satisfying
- Zero bugs, 60 fps everywhere, perfect responsive experience, Lighthouse Performance ≥ 98

Working Style:
- Baramine is not a coding expert — he expects flawless, production-ready deliverables he can deploy immediately.
- He strongly encourages proactive, senior-level initiative in motion design, micro-interactions, and visual elevation.
- Communication must be clear, structured, professional, and technically precise (English by default).

Pet Peeves:
- Generic templates, stiff animations, average hover states, or “just another nice website”
- Mechanical linear transitions instead of organic motion
- Lack of emotional delight or cinematic quality

What Baramine Values Most:
- Instant emotional impact and visual delight
- Principal-level animation choreography and interaction design
- Navigation and browsing that feel like a true experience
- Technically flawless execution that surpasses most senior developers
- Experiences that feel premium, memorable, and share-worthy
